About Me

I moved to Nashville in 2004. Strangely enough, not for music although it didn't take long to get involved in the music scene. Between shows, art and motorcycles, I've built up what I think is a pretty cool community of friends and family.

I found a passion for coding while working in the motorcycle industry and have since decided to chase after that passion at full force. I've found it to be one of the most rewarding endeavors I've gone after. If there's anything I've discovered about myself, it's that I love solving problems, personal growth and helping people. With a dedication to efficiency and relationships, it feels like I've finally found a career path I can get excited about - and one that allows a constant opportunity for growth.
